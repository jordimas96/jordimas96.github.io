Frontend programmer at Evora IT Solutions, a multinational company based in Munich, Germany, specialized in creating solutions for clients in the medical sector. I was part of a multicultural team, where communication and work were exclusively in English.

The application was developed in Angular with NgRx, and its objective was to facilitate the management of maintenance and replacement of medical devices.

Tasks

  • Participation in the Angular update team, from Angular 12 to Angular 17
  • Analysis of the impact of implementing new features in Angular 17
  • Participation in the conversion of the application to PWA

I participated in the team that updated the Angular version of the application, resolving errors that arose during the process. The migration was from Angular 12 to the most recent version at that time, 17.

I also participated in the conversion of the application to PWA, with the purpose of expanding its accessibility to other devices, beyond the iPads of the technicians of our client company. The update to PWA would also allow it to be installed directly on the devices and work offline, since the client did not always have a connection at the sites where they did the maintenance. The requests of the entered data were cached to be sent to the server when the connection was restored.
